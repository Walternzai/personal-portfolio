Childhood

My childhood was a whirlwind of experiences, a kaleidoscope of colors and emotions. Growing up in Malindi town, I was surrounded by a loving family and a community of friends who shaped my early years. I vividly recall the thrill of exploring the neighborhood, the joy of building forts in the backyard, and the comfort of snuggling up with a good book on a rainy day. Childhood was a time of endless possibilities, where imagination reigned supreme. One of my most cherished childhood memories is our annual summer vacations to Gede Beach. We'd spend our days swimming in the lake, hiking through the woods, and roasting marshmallows over a campfire. These memories are etched in my mind, a reminder of the simple pleasures that bring joy.
